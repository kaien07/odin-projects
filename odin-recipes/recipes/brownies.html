Brownies

Source: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/25010/absolutely-best-brownies/

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup self-raising flour
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder (Use Dutch-processed for best results)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (Optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degC. Grease and flour an 8x8-inch or 9x9-inch baking pan.
  2. Beat together sugar and butter in a medium bowl. Add eggs and mix well.
  3. Combine flour, cocoa, and salt; stir into sugar mixture. Mix in vanilla, then stir in walnuts.
  4. Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are firm, 25 to 30 minutes. Cool before cutting into squares.
